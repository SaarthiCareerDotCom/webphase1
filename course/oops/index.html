FAQ

  • What kind of certificate will I receive? A participation certificate or a graded certificate?

    An Audit Level Certificate or Participation Certificate is provided to those who enroll for the course.

    A test is conducted for those students who prefer a Graded Certificate. They are awarded with it based on their performance in the test. Graded certificates showcase the student's grasp over the concepts.

  • What are the prerequisites for this course?

    This course is open to all students and professionals who wish to understand and explore the depths of Object Oriented Programming using C++.

    However, the course is most beneficial for those who know a basic programming language like C for advanced learning.

  • How are training sessions held? Online or classroom sessions?

    Classroom sessions are conducted by our instructors. Their profiles may be viewed in the 'Course Trainers' section above. They will be available at all times during class hours.

  • How many students are accommodated in a batch?

    We believe in effective learning through one to one interaction. Hence, the maximum number of students accommodated in a batch is 60.

  • Is it compulsory to have a laptop for this course?

    No, it is not compulsory to have a laptop for this course. However, there are lab sessions at the end of every theoretical class and having laptops is advantageous since hands on practise is recommended.

  • Can I have a refund if I don't like the course?

    If you find the course to be unsatisfactory, a complete refund may be demanded on or before the first day of commencement of the course.

  • Will I get some notes at the end of the course for revising the concepts?

    Yes, you will be provided with notes from our trainers at the end of the course for practice and learning.

  • What softwares or tools will we use as a part of this course?

    We will be using GitHub for Version Control, CodeBlocks IDE , MINGW Debugger , GCC - Compiler. We may also use Visual Studio for implementation of Case Study.

  • Will you be solving a complete Case Study in the class?

    We will provide a case file and the content required to solve the case. You will have to convert the case into a project which will be evaluated by our team later. We will only assist you in completing the case study.
